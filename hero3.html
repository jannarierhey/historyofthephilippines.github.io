Philippine National Heroes

Marcelo Del Pilar

(October 23, 1857 to December 7, 1899) He was a Filipino painter, sculptor and a political activist of the Philippine Revolution during the late 19th century. He became one of the first recognized Philippine artists.

Juan Luna

(January 6, 1812 to March 2, 1919) She was a Filipina revolutionary who became known as Tandang Sora because of her age during the Philippine Revolution. She was known as the “Grand Woman of the Revolution” and the “Mother of Balintawak”.

Melchora Aquino

He was a ruler of Mactan in the Visayas. Modern Philippine society regards him as the first Filipino hero because he was the first native to resist Imperial Spanish colonization.
